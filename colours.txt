Science Behind Rainbow:
Every little droplet works as a prism which scatters the white sun rays opens up the visible spectrum which contains different colours and we see a beautiful rainbow. Voilet and Red colours are extreme ends of visible spectrum. Voilet has the high frequency and small wavelength while red low frequency and large wavelength.
